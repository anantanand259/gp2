About Institute

Government Polytechnic, Adityapur was established in 1980 and is one of the premier state government technical institutions of Jharkhand. It functions under the Department of Higher & Technical Education, Government of Jharkhand. Located in the industrial hub of Adityapur, Jamshedpur, the institute is well connected by road and rail and provides a conducive academic environment.

Our Mission

To impart quality technical education with strong practical exposure, develop skilled and ethical professionals, and meet the evolving requirements of industry and society. Emphasis on hands-on technical skills and holistic student development.

  • Quality technical education with practical orientation
  • Enhance students' technical skills & professional competence
  • Strengthen industry–institute collaboration

Our Vision

To emerge as a centre of excellence in diploma engineering education, producing competent and responsible professionals capable of contributing to national and industrial growth.

  • Industry-ready and socially responsible graduates
  • Continuous improvement in academic standards
  • Foster innovation and creative thinking
Why Us

Why Choose Government Polytechnic Adityapur

Placement Support

Consistent placement support with participation from reputed core and IT sector industries like Tata Steel, Wipro, JSPL, and L&T.

Affordable Education

State-funded technical education with highly affordable fees for all categories.

Scholarship Facilities

Eligible students can avail state government scholarships including E-Kalyan scheme.

Laboratories & Library

Well-equipped laboratories and a central library supporting practical and academic learning.

Hostel & Campus

Hostel accommodation for boys and girls with essential campus infrastructure. Capacity for 100 students.

Anti-Ragging & Safety

Active Anti-Ragging and grievance cells ensure a safe and disciplined campus environment.
